What is Speak Out Loud?

Fighting for a Cause

In partnership with the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention, Spotify is hosting a multi-day benefit concert to help find better ways to prevent suicide, promote a culture that's smarter about mental health, and bring hope to those affected by suicide.

Suicide is an Epidemic

Statistics surrounding suicide are staggering. Suicide is the 10th leading cause of death in the United States. Each year, nearly 45,000 Americans are lost to suicide. And for every suicide, 25 will make an attempt on their life.[1] Enough is enough.

What is Spotify doing?

No benefit concert will erase the damage done by suicide. But at Spotify, we believe that music helps connect us in ways no other medium can. Speak Out Loud is about pushing out one simple message: you're never alone in this struggle. Together, we can help erase the stigma associated with talking about mental health problems and suicide.
